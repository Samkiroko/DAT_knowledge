Student Registration

For a student you have two registration options either through the social login or registration form.

Activation Email

Upon successful registration, activation email will be sent to your inbox. You will receive two emails welcome email and activation email, click on activate button and you will be redirected back to the main website to login with your registered details i.e. your email and password (In case you forgot your password check your email).

How to Login

  1. Add you registration email
  2. Add you password
  3. Just login you will be re-directed to your student Dashboard
  4. You are ready to start your bookings

Install Mobile App

From the same activation email you have links to install iOS or android apps. You can also click on the footer icon for the installation.

With the app you will be able to:

  • Book a tutor
  • Check your booking status
  • Save your favorite tutor
  • Message tutor
  • Looking for tutor by filling leading form
  • View course offering
  • Add much more..

Booking

All the students booking will display here the status of each booking.

Different booking status

  • Pending: After A new Booking status, tutor should accept your request.
  • Approved: If the Tutor Accepts your Booking Shows this status.
  • Session initiate: Tutor will initiate the session Before Starting of the course (This button will be visible before starting the course set by tutor) .
  • Start Course: After tutor change status to in session Initiate This is enable in student.
  • Running: If the student clicks on the start course the session will start and if its online it will automatically redirect to video conferencing system.
  • Completed: If the course is completed on the updateâ€™s status.
  • Claim for admin Intervention: Student may cancel the booking before starting course, then he/she will be refunded. Note once the course is started then cancelled it is not refundable.
  • Closed:All successfully completed bookings will be listed here.

Enrolled Courses

All Batches that the student is enrolled to under Institute will display here. Student can join the class by clicking on the blue icon, they will be redirected to the video conferencing system if the tutor has approved and started the class.

My Leads

Leads are requirements posted by the students and these requirements are visible to tutors. Post Requirements: Student can post their requirements details Here All the posted requirements are shown on the Teaching jobs tab on the main site. Leads: All posted requirements are shown in Leads.
