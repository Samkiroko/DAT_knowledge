Getting started

As a registered tutor you get access to students’ details who are looking for private teacher, You will be able to contact them with their requirements to have a booking with you. Through the platform tutor will be able to create tutoring course that can be booked by the students. To up sell on the dial a teacher as a tutor the platform has a built downloadable course creator system for either file or video, student will be able to purchase the material on the DAT market place

Downloadable courses: From home page second row.
.

Tutor Registration

Navigate to the login/register tab, on the registration form choose tutor, fill all your details.On successful registration an activation email will be sent.

Activation Email

Upon successful registration, activation email will be sent to your inbox. You will receive two emails welcome email and activation email, click on activate button and you will be redirected back to the main website to login with your registered details i.e. your email and password (In case you forgot your password check your email).

How to Login

  1. Add you registration email
  2. Add you password
  3. Just login you will be re-directed to your tutor Dashboard
  4. You are ready to receive bookings after admin verification is done.

Install Mobile App

From the same activation email you have links to install iOS or android apps. You can also click on the footer icon for the installation.

With the app you will be able to:

  • Accepts booking
  • update booking status
  • View student leads
  • Message student
  • View courses
  • Add much more..

Accept Bookings

All the students booking will display here and as tutor you can update the status.

Different booking status

  • Pending: New booking by student will be displayed here.
  • Approved: All approved booking.
  • Session initiate: tutor will initiate the session before starting the course.
  • Start Course: After changing status to in session Initiate This is enable in student.
  • Running: If student clicks on start course the session will start.
  • Completed: If course completed updates to this section. After completion tutor can request admin for money conversion of credits.
  • Claim for admin Intervention: This status can be updated after starting the course, if there is an issue that needs admin attention.
  • Closed:All successfully completed bookings will be listed here.
