How to enroll to An Institute

I guess now you are set to get started.Searching and choosing an institute on dial a teacher platform will be the first step.

Enrolling to An Institute batch (group class)

After choose the institute you want to enroll to then you have two option buttons

Join Institute buttons Option:

  1. View Profile: On the tab yow will get about Institute, contact detail,course offering and experience details.
  2. Book Now: The tab will direct you to the booking form.

Reserve Your Spot

A detail booking form will be on the section, make sure to fill all the input for a successful request

Reservation Form Details

  • Select A Course:On the input select your preferred course form all the offered listed course by the Institute.
  • Select Batch:On the input you will select the course that you are interested with.
    The specific batch details will show with timing,teaching type start and end date.
  • Select Time-slot:On the option will indicate tutor availability to offer the course.
  • Write Your Message:In case you need to share any details with the tutor before the course start date, it optional.

Request This Tutor

After filling the form, the total cost for the course will show on the top left side, make sure you have enough credit to make the purchase.

Success message will show

Your slot with the tutor has been booked successfully: Once tutor has approved your booking and Initiate the session you can start the course on the booked date.

My Dashboard

After the success message navigate to My Dashboard tap to view the booking status, where the course booking will be pending

Use you phone app to check the update of the course status.
